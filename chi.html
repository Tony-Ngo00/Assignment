Cha Hae In




Personality

Cha is very meticulous and cares about the lives of those around her, as demonstrated by how she developed a habit of patrolling the boss' lair during her guild's raids in order to ensure the safety of the mining and hauling teams. She is also very diligent when training, as she continued to hone her sword technique even after becoming a S-Rank Hunter.

Appearance

Cha Hae In is a pretty young woman with gray eyes and blonde hair in a bob cut. She typically wears red-themed armor on raids and carries a black sword with a silver hilt. When using her abilities, her eyes glow yellow.

Powers and Abilities

Mana Smell: Cha is sensitive to the smell of mana, causing hunters and monsters to smell foul to her. Jinwoo is the only known hunter who does not smell bad to her, due to his status as the Player of the System.

Master Swordsman: Cha is proficient at swordsmanship and is able to come up with creative and unorthodox ways to kill monsters efficiently. As a testament to her sword-fighting skills, she was able to easily defeat Igris during their sparring session.

Enhanced Strength: Cha possesses great strength. She was able to easily defeat Igris in their sparring match, hit Kumamoto in the face with enough force to make him bleed, and decapitate the Ant Queen with one swing of her sword.

Enhanced Speed: Cha is able to move at great speeds and is well-known amongst the Korean hunting community for just how fast she is in battle. She was also able to easily dodge Igris' attacks, pummel Kumamoto before he could even react, overwhelm Kanae with her movements, and even dodge some of Beru's attacks during their sparring match.

Enhanced Durability: Cha possesses a great deal of durability. As a testament to this, she was able to stay alive for a significant amount of time after the Ant King injured her and continue moving at top speed after the Architect cut clean through her shoulder.

Sword Dance: Cha significantly increases her attack speed, giving her dance-like movements.

Sword of Light: Cha transforms her sword into pure light, significantly increasing her cutting power.

Quake of Provocation: Cha stabs her sword into the ground, creating a small earthquake that damages any enemies caught in its radius.
